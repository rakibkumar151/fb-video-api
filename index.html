Questions & Answers

FAQ

Is VidSnap completely free?
Yes! VidSnap is 100% free to use with no hidden charges, no subscription, and no account required.
Does the downloaded video have a watermark?
No watermarks are added by VidSnap. You get the original video exactly as it exists on the platform.
What video quality will I get?
VidSnap fetches the best available quality for each video. For most platforms this is 1080p or the original upload quality.
Why is my Instagram link not working?
Make sure the post is public. Private or restricted content cannot be downloaded. Also ensure the URL is the full post URL, not a shared short link.
Is it legal to download videos?
Downloading videos for personal offline viewing is generally acceptable. Redistributing or monetising downloaded content may violate the platform's terms of service and copyright law. Always respect creators.
